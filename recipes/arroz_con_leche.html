Arroz Con Leche

Description

This is Mexican rice pudding and easy to make

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup rice
  • 2 1/2 cup water
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 3/4 cup evaporate milk
  • 1/2 cup sweeten condensed milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Place water, rice and cinnamon in a saucepan, boil and simmer until rice is tender
  2. Remove the pot from heat, add evaporate milk, condensed milk and vanilla extract
  3. Return the pot to heat and cook until it thickens
