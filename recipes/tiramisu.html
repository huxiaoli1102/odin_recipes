Tiramisu

Description

This is a quick Tiramisu recipe using simple ingredients

Ingredients

  • Lady fingers
  • Coffee
  • Cream cheese 115 g
  • Heavy whipping cream 400 ml
  • Sugar 70 g
  • Cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Make coffee 250 ml
  2. Whip cream, add sugar, add softened cream cheese
  3. Assemble and sift cocoa powder
